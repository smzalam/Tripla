Intro and Goal

My physics teacher in Grade 12 of high school had us do a quiz in the form a game. Namely, the class was divided into two teams, and we took turns back and forth answering physics questions in the form of a battleship game. Whoever got a question right got the right to make a move on the board. It was a very fun experience, and so this app is based on that type of game.

Purpose

With covid times and quarantine making leanring all the more personal, having a system like Tripla at hand makes it easier to incorporate inclusively and interactivity within the classroom. It makes sure everyone is paying attention within their teams and that they can have fun too in a friendly, competitive atmosphere.

Audience

It is for any person interested in making use of it. The purpose of use of Tripla is meant to be very similar to Kahoot, i.e. flexible and not limited to education although education is what the basis of Tripla is.

Features

  1. Creating a new game
    • A screen to enter questions and answers
    • Choosing a type of game (b/w battleship, tic tac toe, and Connect4)
  2. Joining a game
    • If a game has already been made, you can join the room by entering the game code and entering a nickname
    • Modified rules of games to accomodate for participant numbers.
  3. Game Screen with teams and a game board
    • The final screen where you actually play the game
    • Each team can only see its own board
    • Click on buzzer to answer and make a move on the board if answer is correct.
